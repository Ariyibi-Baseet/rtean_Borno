Chairman Remarks

WELCOME TO THE HOME OF PEACE. I wish to specially welcome you to the official website of the Borno State Chapter of the Road Transport Employers Association of Nigeria (RTEAN). We are not just the movers of the Nation's Economy, we are also the foremost transportation Union in the Federal Republic of Nigeria. Though the main stay of Borno's Economy is Agriculture, particularly crop and livestock farming, since the commencement of our operations in this state in 19.........., we have played fundamental roles in the development of the state, providing comfortable, safe, reliable and efficient means of mobility to all and sundry, to and fro every part of Borno State -- the Home of Peace while ensuring a stable means of livelihood to hundreds of people across the nooks and crannies of the state. On this platform, we assure you of an up-to-date information about our activities, our various parks as well as our partnership with the Borno State Government ably led by our visionary and performing Governor, His Excellency, Prof Babangana Umara Zulum. In case of any complaints, suggestions or enquiry, you can visit our office or call any of the telephone lines herein provided. As you continue to navigate your way within the state, to and from any part of the country, please be assured that as your dependable partner in progress, we are set to safely take you to your destination.
Once again, welcome to the Home of Peace. UP Road! Super UP Road!! Maturity UP UP Road!! Unity and Progress.
Sincerely Yours
Ambassador Muhammad Abubakar Bishara Chairman, RTEAN,Borno State Branch.
